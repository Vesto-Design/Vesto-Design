### 🚀 About

Mechatronics and robotics engineer working on robotics, SLAM, and autonomous
systems. Most of my time goes into reinforcement learning for autonomous
navigation, alongside research and publishing. I also come from motion design, so
I like building where robotics meets human centered design. Open to collaborations
in automation, robotics, and machine learning.
